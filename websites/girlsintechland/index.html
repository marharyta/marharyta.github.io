Registration

Due to a limited amout of seats on the workshop, we propose you to pass the tests that we created for you. Those tests are meant to verify you have a sufficient starting level of HTML&CSS (JavaScript - only basic things) to take the most advantage of our workshop. To submit your tasks, please crete an account on codepen.io and crete code snippets ('pens') with your code - then save them and attach links to your application in the appropriate field. Good luck!
