.Aero is part of Hack Junction, a 48-hour hackathon running on November 6-8th in Suvilahti, Helsinki.

WHAT IT'S ABOUT

Are you like us? Do you believe air travel needs a shake-up? Do you have the vision, ambition and skills to be part of the solution? Then, you will love #PaxExHack

We are gathering over one hundred of the world’s best developers, designers and entrepreneurs - and providing them with data, APIs and everything they will need to create the next generation of air travel services. Finnair, Reaktor and Finavia are serious about shaking up the industry. Big innovations are on the cards in Helsinki from the 6th to the 8th of November.

If you did not make it before the registration deadline, stay tuned! The final pitches will be shown via Periscope, and more hacks are in the horizon.

We are serious about shaking up the industry, so keep an eye on #PaxExHack throughout the event to get a piece of the action.

Want to know what we will be looking for? Read more in our post on the Junction Blog.

INTERNATIONAL APPLICANTS

Thank you all for your video entries. Our selection panel is working overtime to be able to get back to you as soon as possible. Selected teams / participants will be notified by Tuesday 27th of October.
